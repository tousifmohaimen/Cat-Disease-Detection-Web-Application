About Us

Welcome to MyCat

MyCat is designed to help cat owners maintain and organize their cat's physical health. There are cat owners that still handle and manage their cat’s without knowing that their cat is having a disease or not. This can lead to negligence of the cat’s health and may result to more fatal disease or even worse death. Our software is easy to use for both admin and cat owner. It can record and display the results of each cat registered.
