About Us

Welcome to MyCat

In the era of rapid development of the internet, where pet owners are now so enthusiastic to see their petsâ€™ behaviour and capture this moment through the camera. Animal owners also often use the internet to access various articles on the internet to recognize the disease's nature, behaviour, and symptoms. However, some articles are not actual and true for the health of their pets. Animal owners need a good source and useful media to see the condition of their pets. Thus, we developed a system that focused on cats that could allow users to find ways to prevent and perform the first action on their beloved cat when strange symptoms seen in cats or cats are already infected by an illness as well as providing tips and tricks in the form of actual information in the care of cats. Therefore, this application can be a web application or a media to help cat owners do the right action based on the illness suffered in the form of useful and actual information for their cat. Therefore, the formulation of our problem in making this research is how cat owners can know the diseases suffered by cats based on visible symptoms, how cat owners can find information about handling the disease, first aid on what to do when the cat is sick, how cat owners can find information about the prevention of diseases that must be done when the cat is sick and how cat owners can find out information about tips and tricks in cat care using the system. From the problems in, it can be concluded that our goal in making this system is to create an application that can help cat owners to know the type of cat disease based on visible symptoms, provide information that can help cat owners to know easily about the handling and first aid against the cat, providing information that can help cat owners find out easily about cat's disease prevention and provide information on the system that can help cat owners find out easily about tips and tricks in cat care. The benefit of the system is, cat owners can rapidly diagnose cat diseases, accurately and interactively. It helps cat owners to take early action against diseases suffered by cats, provides detailed information on cat disease prevention, and increases knowledge to the owner about tips and tricks in cat care.
